California Melt

Description

A satisfying open-faced vegetarian sandwich with avocado, almonds and mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grained bread, lightly toasted
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup slices toasted almonds
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven broiler
  2. Lay the toasted bread out on a baking sheet. Top each slice of bread with 1/4 of the avocado, mushrooms, almonds, and tomato slices. Top each with a slice of Swiss cheese.
  3. Broil the open-face sandwhiches until the cheese melts and begins to bubble, about 2 minutes. Serve the sandwhiches warm.
